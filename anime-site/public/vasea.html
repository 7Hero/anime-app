Frequently asked questions

How does the 14-day trial work?

We offer a free 14-day trial for all Avocode subscriptions upon sign up. No credit card required.

How do I pay for your service?

Avocode charges for each user who works with design files. At the end of your free trial, you will be asked to enter your billing information to continue using the service. You may choose between Monthly and Yearly billing.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can upgrade, downgrade, pause or cancel your plan at any time. You will lose access to Avocode at the end of your subscription period.
